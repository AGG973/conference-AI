About Us

A group of students from the British University of Bahrain/Salford Manchester formed the VISION company to address core knowledge and topical areas in IT and technology AI, and they are eager to host this conference to raise the profile of both the University and the City of Salford as major players in the world of information technology.

What is Our Goal?

Our purpose for this conference is to create a common foundation for what AI means to businesses and the future of our planet as we approach a new era of ethical human-machine partnership.As we guarantee that:

  • Our speakers are at the forefront of AI, business, and innovation.
  • Engaging with the brightest minds in AI and business, as well as forming alliances and friendships with others.
  • Obtaining the most current technologies
  • How to leverage AI to generate outstanding content in your daily routines.

Is hotel accommodation included in the registration fee?

The registration fee does not include the cost of hotel accommodations or airfare. Participants are responsible for their own lodging and travel expenses. Please see the event website for hotel information and instructions on how to reserve your lodgings.

Can I pay for my conference registration when I arrive at the meeting?

We advise all attendees to pre-register for the conference. This allows us to plan for the right number of participants and arrange any necessary accommodations.

Who is eligible to attend the Welcoming Reception?

The Welcoming Reception on Saturday night is open to all conference attendees. Your registration includes attendance.

Will there be possibilities for networking during the event?

Yes. The programs will include breakout sessions for participant discussions as well as interactive activities.
